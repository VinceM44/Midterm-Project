Fort Hays Municiple Golf Course

About the Course

Just south of the FHSU campus lies the Fort Hays Municiple Golf Course. Better known as "The Fort" by golfers in the area, "The Fort" offers golfers a challenging experience. With water around half of the holes, and a difficult landscape, one should never stray too far from the fairway. While playing, golfers can enjoy a little bit of history. The golf course was built around a historic millitary outpost, which remnants of can still be seen.
