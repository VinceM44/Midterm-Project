    • About the Course

    After a stop at the pro-shop, players are immediately greeted with a difficult hole. Having to go over water in the fairway, and with out-of-bounds to the left, players will instantly get a feel for the challenges ahead. Although the slopes aren't quite as extreme as the front nine, the fairways are tighter, the holes are longer, and water is more common on the back nine. Walking down some of the fairways can also give you a stunning view of the FHSU campus as you go by.
